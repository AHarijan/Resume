About Me

I am Alakar Harijan, a detail-oriented Data Analyst with 5 years of experience in analyzing and interpreting complex data to drive business insights. Currently working at ISS Shipping India Pvt Ltd, I have honed my skills in statistical analysis, and visualization tools such as Excel, Power BI, and Tableau. Throughout my career, I have successfully led data-driven projects that improved decision-making and operational efficiency. I take pride in my ability to turn raw data into actionable insights that support strategic planning and business growth. I am a continuous learner, always seeking to expand my knowledge and stay updated with the latest data analysis techniques and tools. Beyond work, I am passionate about machine learning and coding, which further fuels my analytical mindset.
